2026 Strategic Analysis

Mexico enters 2026 as the premier choice for North American remote workers. While Medellin and Bogota have seen price increases, your dollar still goes significantly further in Colombia than in almost any other Western-timezone hub. It remains the top choice for those looking to maximize their lifestyle-to-cost ratio.
