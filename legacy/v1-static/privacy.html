Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 17, 2026

1. Who We Are

Nomad Budgeter ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website nomadbudgeter.com. We provide a free digital nomad tax and cost of living calculator. Our contact email is hellonomadbudgeter@gmail.com.

2. Data We Collect

We are committed to minimal data collection:

  • Calculator Inputs: The income, city, and expense values you enter are processed entirely in your browser. They are never sent to our servers or stored in any database.
  • Analytics Data: We may use privacy-respecting analytics (e.g., page views, device type, country). No personally identifiable information (PII) is collected.
  • Cookies: We use only essential cookies required for site functionality. If third-party advertising (e.g., Google AdSense) is enabled, those services may set their own cookies in accordance with their own privacy policies.

3. Global Privacy Control (GPC)

We fully respect the Global Privacy Control (GPC) signal. If your browser sends a GPC signal (Sec-GPC: 1), we treat it as a legally binding opt-out of any data sale or sharing under applicable laws including GDPR, CCPA, and the 2026 EU Digital Services Act amendments.

When a GPC signal is detected:

  • No third-party advertising cookies will be loaded.
  • No data will be shared with third-party analytics.
  • Only essential, first-party functionality cookies will remain active.

4. Third-Party Services

Our calculator uses the following third-party APIs to provide real-time data:

  • API-Ninjas: For city data and income tax calculations. Their privacy policy is available at api-ninjas.com/privacy.
  • ExchangeRate-API: For real-time currency conversion. Their privacy policy is available at exchangerate-api.com.

Your income and city inputs are sent directly from your browser to these APIs over encrypted HTTPS connections. We do not act as an intermediary or store this data.

5. AI Disclosure

Portions of this website's calculation logic and content were developed with the assistance of AI tools. All outputs have been human-reviewed and verified against 2026 tax and regulatory data. AI is used for real-time accuracy enhancement, not for making autonomous financial decisions on your behalf.

  • Request deletion of your data.

  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hellonomadbudgeter@gmail.com.

7. Children's Privacy

Nomad Budgeter is not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.
