Specific Tax Program Disclosures

Our calculator references the following tax programs and visa categories. The rates and rules for these programs may change without notice:

  • Spain — Beckham Law / Digital Nomad Visa: The 24% flat tax rate referenced is based on the 2026 Non-Resident Income Tax (IRNR) regime. Eligibility requirements, minimum income thresholds, and social security obligations may vary.
  • Portugal — D8 Visa / NHR Regime: The 20% flat tax rate referenced is based on the Non-Habitual Resident (NHR) program. Note: Portugal's NHR program has undergone significant changes since 2024. Verify current eligibility with a Portuguese tax advisor.
  • UAE — Freelance / Remote Work Visa: The 0% personal income tax rate is accurate as of 2026. However, UAE introduced a 9% corporate tax in 2023. Determine whether your work structure qualifies as personal or corporate income.
  • Indonesia — B211A / Digital Nomad Visa: The 0% tax on foreign-sourced income is applicable under specific visa categories. Working locally for Indonesian clients may trigger local tax obligations.

Professional Advice Strongly Recommended

We strongly recommend consulting with a qualified tax professional in both your home country and your target destination before making any relocation decisions. Key areas requiring professional guidance include:

  • Tax treaty benefits between your home country and destination
  • Double taxation avoidance agreements
  • Social security and healthcare contribution obligations
  • Permanent Establishment risk assessment
  • Exit tax and departure obligations from your home country
